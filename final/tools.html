Wheels Paint Interior
Microfiber Applicator pads are used for many things in your car wash. From applying Tire gel or applying a coat of wax. You'll definetely need these!
These microfiber Wash mitts are essential to your car washing routine. They do not scratch your paint and easily pick up dirt
A Microfiber drying towel is essential! You wouldn't want to put in all that hard work to just have water spots after or scratch your paint with a normal towel!
This bulk pack of Microfiber towels will come in handy many times. From wiping off excess tire gel to wiping your interior these will always help
While this bucket is branded, that is totally a personal choice. You can pick up some cheap 5 gallon buckets at your local hardware store!
Grit-Gaurds are a newer product I have discovered. They help trap that dirt in the bucket, keeping it from getting back into your Mitt.
I like this wheel brush because the bristles aren't too tough and the handle extendes out so you don't bang up your knuckles on your rims.
This Wheel brush comes in handy for all the spots your normal one cant, it can be bent to reach on the back of the rims not accesible by hands.
While I use the microfiber applicator pad, some people prefer these for their ability to hold gel. The foam seems to get torn up to much for my preferance though.
