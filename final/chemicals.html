I prefer Meguirs Wash and Wax because it has wax in its formula, so if you dont have time for a long wax, your car will still have a good shine.
I use Meguirs Interior cleaner becuase it is safe on all materials so I dont have to worry about it getting on a unsafe material.
I use Meguirs Hot Rims Wheel and Tire becuase like the other products it is safe on multiple metal types. Leaving the guess work out of it.
I use Meguiars Endurance Tire Gel becuase it has not slung onto my paint yet, and it has provided a excellent shine!
I do not have a preferance on wheel wax but this one is easy to use and has great reviews on many websites!
Rain-X glass cleaner is a absolute must! It has the Rain Repellant in it which is a game changer during a rain storm!
