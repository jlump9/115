Web Site Topic Approval!

What is the purpose of the site?

To inform people how to properly wash a vehicle and explaining the benefits of taking care of your vehicle on a regular basis.

What do I want the website to accomplish?

To teach people how to properly wash a car using methods and tools that are not harmful to the paint or materials of the car. ALso to motivate the audience to take care of their vehicle for the benefits explained.

Who is the intended audience?

Male that is teen or young adult.

What issue does this website address?

People not knowing how to wash a car without damaging the clear coat or unintentionally harming and decaying the materials.

What type of content is included in the site?

Proper tools for cleaning, cleaning methods for different areas (wheels, engine, body, interior, etc), and a motorcycle section.
